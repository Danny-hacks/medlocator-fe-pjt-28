What Does MedLocator do?
For Patients

MedLocator helps patients who have drug prescriptions look for their various drugs in pharmacies are nearby their current location. It is a very effective way to save patients in need of drugs the stress of walking into different pharmacy stores to get drugs they need.

For Pharmacies

MedLocator also helps pharmacy store owners to let patients know about them. Pharmacies creates accounts to update the meds they currently have in stock to help patients know the meds they have or not.

Use your current location to find the closest pharmacy store to you when you are in need of your meds.
